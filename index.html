C-Camp Project

C-Camp is a 4EU+ project aimed at stimulating engagement with Technology.
Come and join us to explore Artificial Intelligence, Art, consciousness and creativity.

C-Camp offers a chance to experiment with tools from AI, art and media, privacy and programming.

It combines this with the latest perspectives from the sciences about what machine learning is and why it works,
how psychology and biology interface with machines
and how technology can help us to learn and be more human with each other.

WORKSHOP

Workshop in Prague, Online Showcase

C-Camp starts with a 3-day workshop in Prague where we will explore what Artificial Intelligence, Data and multimedia technologies mean for science, art and industry.

You will have the chance to create your own AIs, analyse your own data, and learn new skill in media production.

No previous experience necessary

This is followed by online team-building leading to an Online Showcase in August 2022

THEMES

Artificial intelligence, natural processes and a changing world

Use AI tools and techniques to explore What is intelligence? How can we work in partnership with machines?

Explore Media production and creative processes, AI assisted media production

Explore How the creative process be changed by AI and how can we tell truth from fiction in the media

Explore Social media and big data

Use text analysis techniques, social media analysis software to understand what Facebook knows about you and how TikTok algorithms work

There are limited funded places for students on C-Camp.
